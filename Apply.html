Why Code-quette?

CONNECTIONS

Build connections with other fellow girls in the field! Through 1:1 advising sessions and guidance based mentorship program, meaningful and personal connections are built as well as life long friendship at UT Austin!

GROWTH

Code-quettes aim to provide an environment where girls can learn together and grow together. We, as Buds and Bud-ees, not only have mentors guide their mentees, but also have the mentors learn from our mentees. We want to be able to have both our Buds and Bud-ees grow and pursue CS with confidence!

ACTIVISM

Code-quette is a safe space for females who want to pursue computer science and want to be surrounded with a supportive and welcoming environment in a male-dominated field. We love our buds and we want to help them while breaking stereotypes one step at at a time!
